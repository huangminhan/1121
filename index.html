Tamkang University Department of Information Management
 
The Department of Information Management at Tamkang University warmly invites students who are passionate about technology, innovation, and management to join our recruitment event! Here, you will experience firsthand the unique features of our program, including practical courses in data analytics, system development, information management, and cross-disciplinary applications. We’ve also arranged for seniors to share their learning experiences, competition achievements, and internship stories—helping you gain a clearer picture of your future academic path and career possibilities. Our event features an interactive exhibition area showcasing exciting works such as AI projects, data visualization demos, and programming creations. Professors and project advisors will also be onsite to meet with you, answer your questions, and guide you through what studying Information Management truly looks like. Whether you're aiming for a career in tech, management, or simply exploring which major fits you best, Tamkang IM is the ideal place to build your expertise and competitive edge. Join us and take your first step into the world of Information Management!
